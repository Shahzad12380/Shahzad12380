Important formulas for Chemistry


  • Formula For Calculating Mole:

    •

Mole is any substance that has 6.022 × 1023of substance also known as Avagardo's number and the formula for calculating mole is this:


Mole = mass in g ÷ Molecular mass

  • Explanation
    •

1) let's assume that we want to find out Mole of 2.0g of hellium

2) First we will take 2.0g

3) Then we will find out molecular mass of hellium which is 4 (you can look at the periodic table for finding out molecular mass)

4) Then we will Divide 2.0g with 4

5) 2.0 ÷ 4 = 0.5

6) So, 2.0g of hellium has 0.5 Mole


  • Formula for Calculating Mass in gram:

    •

To find out Mass in gram of any Atom this is the formula:


Mass in gram = Mole × Molar Mass

  • Explanation
    •

1) let's suppose that we want to find 1.2 moles of k

2) First we will find out Molecular mass of k which is 39

3) Then we will multiply 1.2 mole with Molecular Mass

4) 39 × 1.2 = 46.8g

5) So, 1.2 Moles of k has 46.8 grams
